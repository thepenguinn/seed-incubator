\section{Air Moisture Monitoring System} \label{sec:}


The \emph{air moisture monitoring system} also make use of DH22 paired sensors
as described in section \ref{sec:tempMonitoringsystem}. Physically these two
systems\footnote{Thermal and air moisture systems.} make use of same hardware,
but conceptually they can be seen as two different systems. The DH22 can
measure $0-100\%$RH\footnote{Relative humidity.} with $1\%$ tolerance.
