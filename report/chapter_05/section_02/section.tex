\section{Soil Moisture Monitoring System} \label{sec:}

Only the \emph{water content} of the soil will be monitored, and controlled. The \emph{fertilizer}
will be dispatched in a periodic manner. The monitoring system consists of \emph{soil moisture sensors}
that are placed right below where the seed will be.
